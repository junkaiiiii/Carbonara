Our Policies

Safety First

  • All users must verify their identity with valid ID and phone number
  • Both drivers and passengers can rate each other after trips
  • Report system for any safety concerns or inappropriate behavior

Cancellation Policy

  • Passengers can cancel up to 24 hours before departure for free
  • Drivers must provide 48 hours notice for ride cancellations
  • Frequent cancellations may affect user ratings and account status

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a driver?

Sign up with a driver account, complete your profile verification, and you can start posting rides immediately. We'll verify your driver's license and vehicle information.

Is RideShare safe?

Yes. All users are verified with government-issued ID and phone numbers. We have a rating system and 24/7 support team. You can also share your trip details with trusted contacts.

What if I need to cancel?

You can cancel through the app. Passengers should cancel at least 24 hours before departure, and drivers should give 48 hours notice. This helps everyone plan better.

Can I bring luggage?

Yes, but please coordinate with your driver. Mention your luggage requirements when requesting a ride, and drivers will let you know if they have space.

What if there's an issue during the ride?

Contact our 24/7 support team immediately through the app. We take all safety concerns seriously and will take appropriate action to ensure your wellbeing.
